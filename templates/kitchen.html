Role In Food

Turmeric adds flavor to food, which explains its presence in curry powder. However, turmeric can also play an important role in digesting the food. The spice can contribute to healthy digestion as a result of its antioxidant and anti-inflammatory properties.

Ways to eat and drink turmeric

Add it to scrambles and frittatas.

Toast it with roasted vegetables

Add it to rice

Try it with soups and beans

Make Tea

Blend it into a smoothie
